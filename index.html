What do we do?

We’re excited to use artificial intelligence to craft personalized stories that truly connect with people facing different challenges. By understanding their emotional and psychological needs, our AI will help create stories that offer genuine support, empathy, and practical advice. Our aim is to use these AI-generated narratives to make a positive difference in people’s lives, helping them feel understood and empowered as they navigate their struggles. The children can pick and select the types of stories they want.

How does this work?

We will engage children by creating personalized stories based on their suggestions and experiences. Using advanced language models and the Langchain framework, we will craft narratives that address their specific issues and interests. These custom stories will be both interactive and relevant, allowing children to play and listen to tales that resonate with their own lives, making the storytelling experience more meaningful and impactful.
